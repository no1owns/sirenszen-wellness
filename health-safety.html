Health & Safety

We are committed to providing a clean, safe, and comfortable environment for every client. The following protocols are in place at every session.

  • Massage table sanitized before and after each client
  • Appointments scheduled with 1 hour cleaning time between sessions
  • Masks worn by therapist — optional for clients once on the table
  • Clients may remain face down (prone) for the majority or all of their session if preferred
